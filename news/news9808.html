  • [30 August 1998] Two short sutta translations:
    • Naga Sutta (Ud 4.5) — The Bull Elephant (John D. Ireland, trans.)

  • [29 August 1998] Ajaan Lee's books are now readable online All the books and talks by Ajaan Lee have been converted to HTML. You may read them online, or download the entire collection .
  • [27 August 1998] "Ven. Balangoda Ananda Maitreya: A Personal Appreciation" Ven. Bhikkhu Bodhi recounts with fond appreciation his years with the late Ven. Ananda Maitreya (1896-1998), Sri Lanka's highest-ranking and widely-venerated scholar-monk.
  • [13 August 1998] Ajaan Mun's talks are now readable online
  • [8 August 1998] Upasika Kee Nanayon's talks are now readable online The collected Dhamma talks of Upasika Kee Nanayon, one of this century's great meditation masters from the Thai forest tradition, are now available for online browsing. Her talks have recently been revised and corrected in preparation for the September reprinting of the anthology An Unentangled Knowing: The Teachings of a Thai Buddhist Lay Woman
  • [3 August 1998] A Handful of Leaves making good progress... On 31 July I sent a pre-release version of the Handful of Leaves CD-ROM to several reviewers for their comments. Barring any major obstacles, the planned release date of 1 December now seems very realistic (perhaps it will be ready even earlier). Many thanks to all of you who have sent in your postcards; your interest and support for this project continue to be an inspiration to me.
