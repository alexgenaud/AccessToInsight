  • [24 Dec 96] A new way to read Dhamma books online? The book Lay Buddhist Practice, by Bhikkhu Khantipalo, has been available for download in plain text form for some time now. I've just finished putting together an online hypertext edition of the book, for users with Web browsers (such as Netscape) that support "frames" . Please try it out and let me know what you think. Is this a useful format for Dhamma books? Is it easy to use? Do you have any suggestions for making it better? I welcome your feedback.
