Thag 15.1
PTS: Thag 675
Aññakondañña Thera: Aññakondañña
(excerpt)
translated from the Pali by
Andrew Olendzki
© 2005
... Just as a rain-cloud would settle The dust that's been raised by the wind, So all conceptions come to rest — When one sees clearly with wisdom. ...
