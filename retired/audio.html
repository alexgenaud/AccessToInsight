The links to external websites on this page were deactivated in late 2013, as they are no longer supported on Access to Insight. The URLs are displayed here as a convenient starting point for users who may wish to build and maintain their own lists of such links.

Suttas in English  

  • Ancient Buddhist Texts <http://www.ancient-buddhist-texts.net/Audio/AU-index.htm> (Malaysia; MP3): Audio recordings of suttas translated by Anandajoti Bhikkhu.
  • Audio Tipitaka <http://audtip.org> (MP3): Sutta readings in Pali, English and more. A wiki-style collection of audio recordings of sutta readings.
  • SuttaReadings <http://www.suttareadings.net> (USA; MP3): Suttas selected and read aloud by Theravada teachers.

Pali chanting  

  • BuddhaNet Audio <http://www.buddhanet.net/audio-chant.htm> (Australia; RealAudio and MP3): A collection of chants from various Theravada traditions (in Pali and English).
  • Chant Pali <http://www.chantpali.org> (MP3): A collection of Pali chants, with pronunciation guides and translation (in Pali and English).
  • Dhamma Talks <http://www.dhammatalks.org/chant_index.html> (USA; MP3): Pali chanting by the Wat Metta Sangha

Dhamma talks (downloads and streams)  

  • Abhayagiri Monastery <http://www.abhayagiri.org/index.php/main/media> (USA; MP3 and RealAudio): Dhamma talks by monastics from Abhayagiri.
  • Aruna Ratanagiri <http://ratanagiri.org.uk> (Harnham Buddhist Monastery, Northern UK; MP3): Dhamma talks by the resident monks: Ajahn Munindo (abbot), Ajahn Abhinando and Ajahn Punnyo.
  • Audio Dharma <http://www.audiodharma.org/> (USA; MP3): An extensive catalogue of Dhamma talks by more than two dozen western teachers, including Thanissaro Bhikkhu <http://www.audiodharma.org/talks/ThanissaroBhikkhu.html>.
  • Ayya Khema Talks <http://ayyakhematalks.org> (USA; MP3): More than 400 Dhamma talks by the late Ayya Khema.
  • Beyond the Net <http://www.beyondthenet.net/AUDIO/Audio_Main.asp> (Sri Lanka; MP3): Sutta discussions with Bhikkhu Bodhi and Dhamma talks by several Sri Lankan teachers.
  • Bhavana Society <http://www.bhavanasociety.org/list/category/MP3s/> (USA; MP3; Podcast): Dhamma talks by Bhante H. Gunaratana and Bhante Y. Rahula given to the Bhavana Society community and to visitors during retreats.
  • Bodhi Monastery <http://www.bodhimonastery.net/bm/about-buddhism/audio.html> (USA; MP3): Bhikkhu Bodhi's excellent series of ten lectures on the Buddha's teachings, as well his weekly lectures on selected suttas from the Majjhima Nikaya.
  • BuddhaNet Audio <http://www.buddhanet.net/audio-talks.htm> (Australia; RealAudio and MP3): Dhamma talks by a wide assortment of teachers.
  • Buddhist Society of Western Australia <http://www.bswa.org> (Australia; MP3): Dhamma talks by several western monastics and senior Thai monks.
  • Corporate Body of the Buddha Educational Foundation <http://www.budaedu.org/ghosa/C016/> (Taiwan; MP3): Dhamma talks by Bhikkhu Bodhi and others.
  • Dhammadownload <http://www.dhammadownload.com/AudioInEnglish.htm> (Singapore; WMA & MP3): Dhamma talks by Burmese sayadaws and lay teachers.
  • Dhammasala Buddhist Monastery <http://dhammasala.org> (USA; MP3): Dhamma talks by Ajahn Khemasanto.
  • Dhamma Talks <http://www.dhammatalks.org/> (USA; MP3): Dhamma talks by Thanissaro Bhikkhu of Metta Forest Monastery.
  • Dharmaseed.org <http://www.dharmaseed.org> (USA): A nonprofit publisher and distributor of recordings of meditation instructions and teachings by teachers from the Insight Meditation Society <http://www.dharma.org>.
  • Dharmastream <http://www.dharmastream.org/> (USA; MP3): Dhamma talks by the teachers from the Insight Meditation Society <http://www.dharma.org> (USA).
  • eDhamma: A Window to Theravada Buddhism <http://www.edhamma.com/> (USA; RealAudio): Dhamma talks by Burmese teachers.
  • Forest Dhamma Books <http://www.forestdhammabooks.com> (Thailand; zipped MP3): Dhamma talks by Ajahn Paññavaddho.
  • The Forest Hermitage <http://foresthermitage.org.uk/dhamma-talks/> (UK; Windows Media Audio): Dhamma talks by Ajahn Khemadhammo.
  • Forest Monastery of Barn Tard <http://www.luangta.com/English/site/audio.php> (Thailand; RealAudio & Windows Media Audio): Dhamma talks by Ajaan Maha Boowa.
  • Forest Sangha <http://www.forestsangha.org/> (UK; CDs, MP3) is a portal page for the monastic communities of disciples of Venerable Ajahn Chah, particularly in the West. It offers links to recorded talks by teachers in that tradition.
  • Forest Sangha Audio <http://fsaudio.org> (UK; MP3) Dhamma talks in MP3 format by the European monastic communities of disciples of Venerable Ajahn Chah.
  • Pariyatti <http://pariyatti.org/podcasts/> (USA; MP3): Dhamma Podcasts from S.N. Goenka and others.
  • Sitavana/Birken Forest Monastery <http://birken.ca> (Canada; MP3): Thousands of Dhamma Talks for download, all in the Theravadan Buddhist Thai forest tradition.
  • Tara Brach <http://www.tarabrach.com/audiodharma.html> (USA; MP3): Dhamma talks by Tara Brach.
  • Tathagata Meditation Center <http://www.tathagata.org/> (USA; MP3, RealAudio): Dhamma talks by Sayadaws U Pandita, U Silananda, and Beeling.

Dhamma talks (cassettes and CDs)  

  • Audio Dharma <http://www.audiodharma.org/> (USA): An extensive catalogue of Dhamma talks by more than two dozen western teachers, including Thanissaro Bhikkhu <http://www.audiodharma.org/talks/ThanissaroBhikkhu.html>.
  • Bhavana Society <http://www.bhavanasociety.org> (USA; MP3 CDs): A series of Dhamma talks by Bhante H. Gunaratana from meditation retreats he conducted at the Bhavana Society.
