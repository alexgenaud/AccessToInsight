Google CSE - Standard
© 2010

Google CSE (Standard & GSS) comes in 3 flavors, according to how the search results are presented:

search element
Results are inserted inline.
iFrame
Results are inserted in an iframe on a different page.
Google hosted
Results are hosted on Google's site.
